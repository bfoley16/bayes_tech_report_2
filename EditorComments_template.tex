\section*{Recommendation}

This is the section where (for a journal article) you would typically write a short statement about whether you would suggest the journal ``Accept" the paper, or propose a ``Major revision", ``Minor revision", or ``Reject". Keep this \textbf{brief}. Provide a short rational for your decision, and point the editor toward the major and minor comments section of the Author Comments document for details. \\

\noindent While this decision is \textit{shared} with the authors in a typical peer-review setting, it \textbf{will not be} for this course. As such, be honest and critical, but be fair. In its current state, would the technical report you reviewed be appropriate for consumption by the broader statistical community (think: other students in STAT capstones)? Why or why not?
